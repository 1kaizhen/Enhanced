Foundation

Surfaces

The defining material is .glass — a 30% dark wash with a 36px blur and 1.6× saturation. Stacked over the wallpaper, it stays legible without hiding the image. Strong glass is reserved for modals and menus where the content behind needs more separation.

Variants
Standard glass
background: var(--glass)
backdrop-filter: blur(36px) saturate(1.6)
border: 1px solid var(--line)
border-radius: var(--radius)
Strong glass
background: var(--glass-strong)
backdrop-filter: blur(36px) saturate(1.6)
border: 1px solid var(--line-strong)
border-radius: var(--radius)
When to use which
  • · Standard glass — todo panel, notes panel, popovers, content containers
  • · Strong glass — settings modal, engine picker dropdown, anything floating above another glass layer
  • · No glass — sticky notes (paper feel), dock tile interiors (tinted gradient), the page wallpaper itself
